WHAT SENT ME HERE?

In previous explorations of this place, I noticed some aircraft already painted in artistic ways. When I later did some research, I found out that it was part of an overall project in collaboration with The Smithsonian.

Seeing how much potential doing this has, I decided to create my own little group with some friends to try it out for ourselves. We hope to add our own unique flare to our project planes.

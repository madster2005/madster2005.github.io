"Evolution of Flight"
I've been really interested in planes as of late, so this GIF exists because of that. I took a fighter planes from 4 major periods of history (World War 1, World War 2, Cold War, and modern day), and put them together in a way to make it seem as they are all flying together in chronological order.


"Changing Sky"
In my Spring semester of 2025, I took a photography class. I found I took a lot of pretty photos of the sky, mainly at sunset. This GIF is a mashup of those pictures I took, in a slide-show style.



"Flight off the Page"
I created this GIF in Procreate on my iPad. While I've used Procreate a lot for drawings I do for fun, I've never used it's basic animation software. I chose to do a Budgie flying off a page, as I love birds. It's a rougher style, but that is partly intentional.
